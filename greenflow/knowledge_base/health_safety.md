# Health & Safety Rules

## Environmental Risk Exposure
- **High Risk Score (>80)**: Direct threat to respiratory health. Immediate evacuation from highly congested areas is recommended.
- **Moderate Risk Score (40 - 80)**: Increased risk for children and elderly. Limit long periods of outdoor exertion.
- **Low Risk Score (<40)**: Safe for all general outdoor activities.

## Safety Recommendations
1. Keep windows closed during high risk (>80) alerts.
2. Use air purifiers indoors when outdoor AQI is above 100.
3. Stay hydrated to help your body manage environmental stress.
