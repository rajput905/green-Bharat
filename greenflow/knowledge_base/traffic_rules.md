# Traffic & Environmental Rules

## Vehicle Emission Zones
- **Zone A (Central)**: Only electric or low-emission vehicles allowed when Risk Score is >70.
- **General Traffic**: Heavy vehicles are restricted from entering the city center during peak hours (08:00 - 11:00 and 17:00 - 20:00).

## Congestion Management
- If Traffic Congestion Score exceeds 80%, traffic signals will prioritize clearing main arteries to reduce idling emissions.
- Carpooling is mandatory for government officials when AQI is >150.
